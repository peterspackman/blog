Equilibrium Crystal Shape

Intersection of half-planes

Drag the points in the γ-plot to adjust surface energies!

The Wulff construction:

  • Each surface energy γ defines a half-plane at distance γ from origin
  • The equilibrium shape is the intersection of all half-planes
  • Lower energy surfaces (closer to origin) create larger facets
  • High energy surfaces may not appear if neighbors have lower energy
  • The shape is always convex and minimizes total surface energy
